Employee Attrition Insights

Created a Power BI dashboard to identify attrition drivers using HR survey and exit data. Core findings: remote work reduced average attrition from 0.80 to 0.40; 82.86% of employees reported no innovation opportunities; poor work-life balance and declining reputation (attrition rising ~38% from Excellent to Poor) were top predictors of exits. Provided quantified evidence for HR interventions on flexibility, compensation equity, and engagement.

Tools Used: Power BI
